AS equations for acids and derivatives, nitrogen compounds, polymerisation, and high-frequency ionic forms.

18 Carboxylic acids and derivatives

Preparation of carboxylic acids

  • From primary alcohol. \( \ce{RCH2OH + 2[O] -> RCO2H + H2O} \)
  • From aldehyde. \( \ce{RCHO + [O] -> RCO2H} \)
  • Nitrile, acid hydrolysis. \( \ce{RCN + 2H2O + H+ -> RCO2H + NH4+} \)
  • Nitrile, alkaline hydrolysis. \( \ce{RCN + H2O + OH- -> RCOO- + NH3} \)
  • Ester, acid hydrolysis. \( \ce{RCOOR' + H2O <=> RCOOH + R'OH} \)
  • Ester, alkaline hydrolysis. \( \ce{RCOOR' + OH- -> RCOO- + R'OH} \)

Carboxylic acid reactions

  • With sodium. \( \ce{2RCO2H + 2Na -> 2RCO2Na + H2} \)
  • With NaOH. \( \ce{RCO2H + NaOH -> RCO2Na + H2O} \)
  • With Na2CO3. \( \ce{2RCO2H + Na2CO3 -> 2RCO2Na + H2O + CO2} \)
  • With NaHCO3. \( \ce{RCO2H + NaHCO3 -> RCO2Na + H2O + CO2} \)
  • Esterification. \( \ce{RCO2H + R'OH <=> RCOOR' + H2O} \)
  • Reduction. \( \ce{RCO2H + 4[H] -> RCH2OH + H2O} \)

19 Nitrogen compounds

Primary amines from halogenoalkanes

  • Equation. \( \ce{RBr + 2NH3 -> RNH2 + NH4Br} \)

Nitriles from halogenoalkanes

  • Equation. \( \ce{RBr + CN- -> RCN + Br-} \)
  • Molecular example. \( \ce{C2H5Br + KCN -> C2H5CN + KBr} \)

Hydroxynitriles

  • Aldehyde. \( \ce{RCHO + HCN <=> RCH(OH)CN} \)
  • Ketone. \( \ce{RCOR' + HCN <=> RC(OH)(CN)R'} \)

Nitrile hydrolysis

  • Acid. \( \ce{RCN + 2H2O + H+ -> RCO2H + NH4+} \)
  • Alkali. \( \ce{RCN + H2O + OH- -> RCOO- + NH3} \)

20 Polymerisation

Addition polymers

  • Poly(ethene). \( \ce{nCH2=CH2 -> [-CH2-CH2-]n} \)
  • Poly(chloroethene). \( \ce{nCH2=CHCl -> [-CH2-CHCl-]n} \)

AS practical / ionic appendix

Common acid + carbonate / hydrogencarbonate forms

  • Carbonate net ionic. \( \ce{CO3^2- + 2H+ -> CO2 + H2O} \)
  • Hydrogencarbonate net ionic. \( \ce{HCO3- + H+ -> CO2 + H2O} \)

Common precipitation equations

  • Silver chloride. \( \ce{Ag+ + Cl- -> AgCl(s)} \)
  • Silver bromide. \( \ce{Ag+ + Br- -> AgBr(s)} \)
  • Silver iodide. \( \ce{Ag+ + I- -> AgI(s)} \)
  • Barium sulfate. \( \ce{Ba2+ + SO4^2- -> BaSO4(s)} \)

Useful gaseous test-generation equations

  • Ammonium + alkali. \( \ce{NH4+ + OH- -> NH3 + H2O} \)
  • Sulfite + acid. \( \ce{SO3^2- + 2H+ -> SO2 + H2O} \)
  • Carbonate + acid. \( \ce{CO3^2- + 2H+ -> CO2 + H2O} \)
